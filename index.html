poemBot

printer + pi + python + poems.

Easy to read, carry in your pocket, and share with your friends: poems printed on receipts are fun!

PoemBot is a Raspberry Pi connected to a thermal printer offering physical prints from a daily poetry website at the touch of a button. It was developed to help promote the Vandal Poem of the Day project which seeks to bring relevant contemporary poetry to University of Idaho and the broader Idaho community.

This repository contains the original VPOD files, plus a complete example implementation using public domain poems.
